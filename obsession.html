Obsession 1: Soccer

I breath and live by soccer. It is really the only sport I can catch myself playing all day long and still not get bored. There are so many things you can learn with a ball like kicking, skill moves, and shooting. All of these skills take emense time to master and are fun to master.

Obsession 2: Video Games

I love many types of video games. They can teach you many skills right from the comfort of your home. Certain games can teach you to think outside the box for solutions to problems related to the game, others teach you to make split second desicions racionally increasing your reaction time exponentailly depending on the game.

Obsession 3: Running

Running is something that most people would find to be not enjoyable for rather obvious reasons, but I'd like to think otherwise. Allthough running can be rather tiring to most people, I find that running can give me a sense of relaxation at times due to how calm it can be. Also when you go run with friends, you can create very close bonds with them while expirenceing new things and making new memories together.
